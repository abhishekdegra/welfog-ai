Offers, Discounts & Vouchers:
Offers and discounts are valid for a limited time and subject to inventory availability. Multiple offers or vouchers cannot be combined in a single transaction. Offers cannot be exchanged for cash.

Offer Refunds:
If a product purchased under a discount or offer is cancelled or returned, the user will only be refunded the actual amount paid. The promotional discount or voucher value will be forfeited.

Anti-Phishing & Fraud Warning:
Welfog or its representatives will NEVER call to demand money for prizes, nor will they ask for your Password, PIN, OTP, or CVV. Beware of fake callers. Always communicate via official emails ending with @welfog.com.

Fraud Prevention & Fake Calls:
Welfog and its authorized representatives will NEVER call, SMS, or email you to ask for your password, OTP, PIN, CVV, or card details. Welfog will never ask you to make payments outside the official app via personal UPI, bank transfer, or cash to claim a prize or unlock an account.

Official Communication Channels:
Official Welfog emails will ONLY come from addresses ending in @welfog.com. Welfog does not provide official support through WhatsApp, Telegram, or other social media platforms. Always use the official Welfog mobile app or verified website for all transactions.

Reporting Fraud & Phishing Attacks:
If you receive a suspicious message, fake offer, or accidentally click a phishing link, do not share any personal details. Change your Welfog password immediately and report the incident with screenshots to legalsupport@welfog.com or through the Help & Support section in the app.
