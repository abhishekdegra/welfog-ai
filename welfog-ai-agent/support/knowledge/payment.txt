Payment Methods & Gateway:
Welfog supports all major payment options including UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets. Payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. Welfog acts as a limited payment agent to facilitate transactions.

Cash on Delivery (COD) Rules:
Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on eligible products depending on the user's pin code. Welfog reserves the right to block or restrict COD payments for high-value orders, payment defaults, or suspicious accounts.

Bank Account Update Rules:
Users can update their bank account details for refunds up to 5 times in a single day. If a transaction is already pending, bank details cannot be changed. If the limit is crossed, users must contact customer support.

Service Charges:
Welfog may charge fees for specific services, such as premium returns or COD handling fees. These service charges will be displayed during checkout and are strictly non-refundable once paid.
